# 1. Purpose

The AI Engineering Framework exists to provide a structured and repeatable engineering system for developing, modernizing and maintaining software products.

Its primary objective is to create, preserve and continuously evolve Engineering Knowledge throughout the entire software lifecycle.

The Framework combines engineering principles, architecture, governance, standards, workflows and Artificial Intelligence into a single coherent engineering methodology.

Rather than prescribing specific technologies, programming languages or tools, the Framework defines how software engineering should be organized to achieve predictable, maintainable and high-quality results.

---

# 2. The Engineering Problem

Software projects rarely fail because engineers are unable to write source code.

They fail because Engineering Knowledge is gradually lost.

As projects evolve, architectural decisions become forgotten, business rules become unclear, documentation diverges from implementation and the original reasoning behind important decisions disappears.

The result is increasing technical debt, reduced maintainability and growing engineering risk.

Artificial Intelligence can significantly accelerate implementation, but acceleration alone cannot solve the problem of lost knowledge.

The AI Engineering Framework addresses this challenge by treating Engineering Knowledge as the primary asset of every software project and by providing a systematic approach for preserving and evolving that knowledge over time.
